Simon Kolker

A PhD candidate at the University of Manchester

Studying the effect of uncertainty on Machine Ethics. As autonomous systems take more responsibility in the real world, their decisions must reflect our values while handling moral and outcome uncertainty.

Find me on GitHub, Google Scholar, the University of Manchester and LinkedIn.

Roles

  • Member of the Autonomy & Verification Network
  • Postgraduate Research Representative at University of Manchester, School of Engineering
  • Associate Fellow with Advance HE
  • Programme Committee Member for the 2026 AAAI workshop on Machine Ethics
  • Organising Committee Member for the Formal Methods and Autonomous Systems (FMAS) workshop at IFM 2026
  • Programme Committee Member for the Formal Ethical Agents and Robots (FEAR) workshop
  • Former Publicity Chair for IEEE Space Mission Challenges for Information Technology and Space Computing Conference (SMC-IT/SCC)
