Simon Kolker

A PhD candidate at the University of Manchester

My PhD project studies the effect of uncertainty on Machine Ethics. As autonomous systems take more responsibility in the real world, their decisions must reflect our values while handling moral and outcome uncertainty.

Find me on GitHub, Google Scholar, the University of Manchester and LinkedIn.
